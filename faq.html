    General Questions

    What is Keinen's core mission?

    Keinen is "Built on Faith," focusing on architecting digital ecosystems with integrity and excellence. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex technology and business efficiency through strategic digital transformation.

    Where does Keinen operate?

    Headquartered in India, Keinen serves a global clientele. We have the capability to deploy and manage digital transformation projects across multiple continents, focusing on regions with high industrial activity.

    Technical Expertise

    What is IT-OT Convergence?

    IT-OT convergence is the integration of information technology (IT) systems used for data-centric computing with operational technology (OT) systems used to monitor events, processes, and devices. This is fundamental to Industry 4.0 and Smart Manufacturing.

    How does Keinen approach industrial cybersecurity?

    We implement Zero-Trust architectures specifically designed for OT environments. We follow international standards like ISA/IEC 62443 and NIST frameworks to ensure that critical infrastructure is protected from evolving cyber threats.

    Do you provide 24/7 support?

    Yes, we offer Managed Services that include 24/7 monitoring and support. Our Network Operations Center (NOC) and Security Operations Center (SOC) ensure your infrastructure remains resilient and performant around the clock.

    Industry-Specific Questions

    Does Keinen support pharmaceutical compliance?

    Absolutely. We specialize in implementing data management systems that are fully compliant with 21 CFR Part 11 and GAMP 5. We provide complete validation support for the lifecycle of the technology.

    What standards do you follow for Manufacturing projects?

    Our industrial architectures are built upon ISA-95 and ISA-88 standards. This ensures that the digital thread is properly maintained from the shop floor to the enterprise level.
